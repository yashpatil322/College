International conference on Sustainable and Futuristic Technologies 2026 (ICSFT-2026) with the theme of "AI Integration for Sustainable Development". The conference will provide a platform for researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to discuss the latest advancements, challenges, and opportunities in integrating AI into different disciplines for betterment of livelihood.

The conference will invite researchers, industrialists, under graduates and post graduates to publish original, innovative and quality research work.

The Conference also includes keynote sessions and poster presentations in multidisciplinary areas by expert academicians, industry persons and professionals.

Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore's scope and quality requirements.

The conference will invite papers in five tracks involving Computer Science, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, Electronics & Telecommunication, Electrical, Mechanical engineering and Management domains.
