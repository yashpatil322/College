About The Institution

Empowering Future
Innovators

The Marathwada Mitramandal’s Institute of Technology (MMIT), Lohgaon was established in 2008. The Institution is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

The Institute is proudly accredited with an “A” Grade by NAAC.

Our vision is to impart quality education for professional excellence and holistic development. We strive to address the expectations of every student through continual improvement and teamwork.
