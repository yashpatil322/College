Submission Guidelines

Paper Format

Submitted papers (.pdf format) must use the A4 IEEE Manuscript Templates for Conference Proceedings.

Length & Originality

  • Submitted papers may be up to 8 pages. Two additional pages may be added for references.
  • Papers must be the original work of the authors and not simultaneously under review elsewhere.
  • IEEE has a strict policy regarding plagiarism. Prior work must be cited appropriately.

Proofreading & Authors

Please proofread your submission carefully. Clear language (US or UK English) is essential.

Note: The author list registered for each submission is final and cannot be changed after the submission deadline.

IMPORTANT: No-Show Policy

At least one author of each accepted paper is required to register and present the paper. Papers not presented (without prior approval) will be removed from the final conference proceedings and IEEE Xplore. No refunds will be issued.

Publication

All accepted, registered, and presented papers in ICCMRAI 2026 will be submitted to IEEE for publication.
